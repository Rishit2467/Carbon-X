## ÔøΩ Project Description

Carbon-X is a decentralized smart contract platform built on the Stellar blockchain using the Soroban SDK. It enables the tokenization, verification, and exchange of carbon credits representing measurable CO‚ÇÇ offsets from certified environmental projects worldwide.

The platform ensures transparency, integrity, and liquidity in the carbon credit market by leveraging blockchain-based immutability and automated smart contract operations. Through Carbon-X, verified carbon credits are tokenized into digital assets that can be securely traded or permanently retired, establishing a trusted and accessible ecosystem for climate action participants, corporations, and individuals.

## üåê Project Vision

Our vision is to transform the global carbon offset ecosystem by creating a transparent, decentralized, and liquid marketplace for verified carbon credits. Carbon-X aims to:

**Promote Environmental Accountability**: Enable verifiable, tamper-proof carbon credit transactions recorded on the Stellar blockchain.

**Enhance Market Transparency**: Eliminate information asymmetry through on-chain verification and public registries.

**Empower Sustainability Projects**: Provide project developers with direct access to a global liquidity pool without intermediaries.

**Ensure Trust and Integrity**: Guarantee that all credits listed, traded, or retired are validated by authorized verifiers.

**Foster Inclusive Participation**: Allow organizations and individuals worldwide to offset their carbon footprint through decentralized, accessible tools.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Key Features

### 1. Carbon Credit Tokenization

- Each carbon credit batch is tokenized as a unique on-chain asset
- Metadata includes: `project_id`, `issuer`, `vintage_year`, `region`, `verified_by`, `quantity`, and `retired` status
- Supports both fungible and non-fungible representations of carbon credits

### 2. Registry & Verification

- Authorized verifiers can register and verify carbon credit batches
- Verification data is stored immutably on-chain for full transparency
- Only whitelisted verifier accounts can perform verification actions

### 3. Marketplace Exchange

- Users can list verified carbon credits for sale in a decentralized marketplace
- Built-in escrow mechanism ensures safe and atomic trade settlement
- Transactions support Stellar Lumens (XLM) and compatible stablecoins

### 4. Retirement System

- Users can retire carbon credits, permanently removing them from circulation
- Retirement actions emit transparent, immutable on-chain events
- Retired credits are viewable via a public retirement ledger for accountability

### 5. Access Control

- Administrative address manages verifier whitelisting and key parameters
- Role-based permissions prevent unauthorized minting or verification
- Ensures governance flexibility for protocol upgrades

### 6. Event Logging & Transparency

- Emits events for all critical actions: verification, minting, listing, sale, and retirement
- Provides an immutable audit trail for regulators, buyers, and verifiers
- Enhances compliance and trust in carbon markets
